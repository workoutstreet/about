Privacy Policy Your Data

We respect your privacy. This page explains what data we collect, how it’s used, and the choices you have.

Last updated: 11 November 2025 This Privacy Policy applies to the Movie Night Planner mobile application (“the App”, “we”, “us”).

1. Information we collect

  • Information you provide – For example, when you contact us by email or send feedback from within the App, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message.
  • Movie night preferences – When you create a “movie night combo” we process your selections (movie/show, genres, mood, group size, budget, time, etc.) so that our AI can generate a personalized plan for you. These preferences are used only to provide and improve the service.
  • Automatically collected data – The App may collect basic technical information such as device model, OS version, app version, and anonymous usage events (for example, which screens are opened) to help us understand performance and improve the experience.
  • Third-party services – The App may use third-party tools (such as analytics, crash reporting or movie information APIs) which can receive certain technical data as part of providing their services.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate and display AI-created movie night plans to you.
  • To operate, maintain and improve the App’s features.
  • To respond to your questions, requests and support emails.
  • To monitor usage, fix bugs and improve reliability and performance.

3. Data storage & security

  • We strive to protect your data and use reasonable technical and organizational measures to secure it.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • Where possible, data used for analytics and crash reports is aggregated or anonymized.

4. Third-party services

The App may integrate with third-party SDKs or APIs. These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

  • Analytics / crash reporting – Used to understand how the App is used and to diagnose technical issues.
  • Movie / TV information APIs – Used to retrieve details such as titles, posters or descriptions to help you discover movies and shows for your night.
  • Advertising (if enabled) – If the App shows ads, some partners may use device identifiers to show more relevant ads, in accordance with platform rules and your consent settings.

5. Children’s privacy

  • Movie Night Planner is intended for general audiences but is not designed to collect personal information from children under the age required by local laws without parental consent.
  • If you believe we have unintentionally collected personal data from a child, please contact us and we will delete it.
